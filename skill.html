In "The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim," skills play a crucial role in shaping your character's abilities and playstyle. The game features 18 skills divided into three main categories: Combat, Magic, and Stealth.

Magic Skills: Center around harnessing magical forces, with skills such as Destruction, Restoration, Conjuration, Alteration, Illusion, and Enchanting. These skills allow you to cast powerful spells, heal wounds, summon creatures, and enhance your gear with magical properties.

Stealth Skills: Emphasize subtlety and precision, including skills like Sneak, Lockpicking, Pickpocket, Light Armor, Speech, and Alchemy. These skills enable you to move undetected, unlock hidden treasures, craft potions, and persuade or barter with NPCs.

Combat Skills: Focus on physical prowess and weaponry, including skills like One-Handed, Two-Handed, Archery, Block, Heavy Armor, and Smithing. These skills enhance your ability to wield weapons, defend yourself, and craft powerful armor and weapons.
